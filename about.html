About The Espresso Cup

I'm Erik — a home coffee nerd and part-time barista-in-my-own-kitchen. I started The Espresso Cup as a way to share my passion for better coffee and help people brew with confidence, curiosity, and creativity.

Whether you're just starting out or already deep into the world of espresso, this blog is your cozy corner for learning, experimenting, and connecting over a shared love of great coffee.

You'll find tips, brew guides, gear breakdowns, and probably a few poetic rants about crema and ritual.

Thanks for stopping by — I hope this little corner of the internet helps make your coffee just a bit more magical.
